Fun Facts about Justin Bieber

  • Justin Bieber was discovered on YouTube in 2008 by Scooter Braun.
  • His debut album "My World" made him the first artist to have seven songs from a debut record on the Billboard Hot 100.
  • Justin is the youngest artist to have five number-one albums in the U.S., a record previously held by Elvis Presley.
  • Justin has a clothing line called "Drew House," known for its comfy, casual style.
